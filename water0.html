About Water Sales Calculator

This application is designed to help water sales businesses calculate their sales, taxes, and total selling prices efficiently. The calculator supports multiple bottle sizes and automatically computes all applicable taxes and charges.

How to Use

1. Enter the quantity of each bottle size you've sold

2. Click "Calculate Total" or wait for automatic calculation

3. View the detailed breakdown of costs, taxes, and the final selling price

Features

  • Support for 0.33L, 0.6L, 1L, and 2L bottle sizes
  • Automatic calculation of Excise Tax (10%)
  • Automatic calculation of Service Charges
  • Automatic calculation of VAT (15%)
  • Real-time updates as you input quantities
  • Multiple theme options
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
